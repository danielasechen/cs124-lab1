The Empty Form

This is the empty form with no tasks. You can enter tasks into the boxes by clicking them and typing. When you click the purple checkbox with a plus or press enter the task will be loaded. When you finish writing a task the checkbox will change so that it is a white empty box.

A form with tasks and some completion

When you complete a task you can click on the blank checkbox and a checkmark will appear to signify that the task is done. Notice that the "add new task" box has moved down with each task that has been added. And the plus checkbox remains.

After pressing one of the bottom buttons

When you click the "Show Only Uncomplete" button, the page will be rendered so that only the uncomplete items (the items whose checkboxes haven't been marked) are shown. To prevent awkward white space, we made it so that the completed items are switched to "display: none;" However, if you delete all completed items (by clicking the button on the bottom right), the completed items will be entirely removed from the html.
